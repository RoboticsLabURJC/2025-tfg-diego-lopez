January was not my most productive month due to the exam period. However, in the limited time available, I focused on testing the **CHAMP algorithm** in different scenarios to evaluate its performance.

First, I tested the algorithm on a **flat surface**. In this scenario, the robot was able to move without major issues, but it showed significant difficulty maintaining a straight trajectory. This behavior was consistent regardless of the direction of motion (forward, backward, or lateral), resulting in a noticeable drift over time.
